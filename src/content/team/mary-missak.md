Dr. Mary Missak has been practicing internal medicine for over 30 years. She graduated from University Of Alexandria Medical School in 1978, completed her residency at Methodist University Hospital, and joined Southaven Internal Medicine in 2000. She received her MBA from Christian Brothers University in 2019 and serves as Chief of Medicine at Baptist Desoto Hospital and medical director for Campus Hospice Southaven division. She has two children who are both in the medical field.
