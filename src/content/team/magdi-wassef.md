Dr. Magdi Wassef has been practicing internal medicine for over thirty years. He graduated from Alexandria Medical School in Egypt in 1985, completed his residency at Methodist University Hospital, and started Southaven Internal Medicine in 1996. He is board certified in internal medicine and is a member of the American College of Physicians and the American Medical Association. He is married to Amira and they have two college aged children, Megan and David.
