Hormones are your body's chemical messengers, affecting everything from your energy and mood to your metabolism and sleep. When they're out of balance, you feel it.

## Signs of Hormone Imbalance

- Fatigue that doesn't improve with rest
- Weight gain, especially around the midsection
- Mood swings, anxiety, or depression
- Difficulty sleeping or poor sleep quality
- Low libido or sexual dysfunction
- Brain fog and difficulty concentrating
- Hair loss or skin changes
